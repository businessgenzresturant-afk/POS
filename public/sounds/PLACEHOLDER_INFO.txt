This directory needs actual MP3 sound files.

Currently, the following files are required:
1. new-order.mp3 - Standard notification beep
2. urgent.mp3 - Urgent alert sound (shorter, more attention-grabbing)

You can:
1. Record your own sounds
2. Download from free sound libraries:
   - https://freesound.org/
   - https://www.zapsplat.com/
   - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/

For testing purposes, you can use any short MP3 files and rename them to:
- new-order.mp3
- urgent.mp3

The system will work without these files (it will fail silently), but sounds won't play.
